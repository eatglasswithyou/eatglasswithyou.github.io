Boriell

is a country in Central and Western Murandar Covering an area of (137,847 sq mi), it lies between the Beaufort and Tellan seas to the north, and the Safi to the south. It borders Fero to the north, Heleta and the Naujan to the east, Bukovce to the south, and Anđelkovska Mahala to the west.

There are 4 states of Boriell, which are controlled under the democratically elected council of Raal, which is comprised of two leaders from every state. Below are these states listed in order of the area, from Silas with the greatest area, to Guin with the least.

Higher Learning

Sinhue

College of Magic
Sinhue was founded during the year 67 by the great Hiltonian witch Avani Espinoza who served as the first head of the school. She was succeeded after her mysterious death by Professor Lester Cortez, who established Sinhue's reputation for emphasizing the necessity of free education.

Sinhue was famous for its acceptance of wild magic sorcery, and was known to have educated (and later expelled) Hawa Parra before his ascension as one of history's most dangerous criminals as leader of the Storm. Professor Bianka Beard, a former Headmaster, was in the service of the last King, Dario Rossi as head mage.
Foer, Silas

Wardens

Univeristy of Might

Founded sometime before the 290s, Wardens has had many noteworthy students over the years. Most prominent were Imaani Salazar, the general who lead the war against the invasion of Bukovice and freed the city of Nanville. Situated in the Deyfall Mountains, visitors speak of the breath-taking beauty of a chateau surrounded by formal gardens and lawns created out of the mountainous landscape by the students as a part of their training. Wardens is dedicated to the physical arts of mind and body, training students in hand to hand combat, weapons, leadership and discipline.

Anwarden, Ostego

Valiant

Trades school

Finding an apprenticeship is always an option, but anyone with a degree from Valiant will never find their shop in need of customers. Valiant teaches the trades. Students at valiant may learn to become more skilled at any number of trades including: midwifery, medical healing, carpentry, construction, wood working, shoe making, cobbling, leatherworking, and the like.

Valiant, Hiltonia

Calidan

Companions school

Calidan is an educational facility for the Companions Guild. All companions must register and attend the univeristy. A Companion is a skilled, well-educated and well-respected member of a guild of professional courtesans/entertainers. Companions are culturally well-educated with training in a number of areas, including psychology, music fencing, and languages, as well as unarmed martial arts.

Mouth, Ostego

Government & History

The time until Bori

Before the council, before Bori came to this land, the area was two separate kingdoms, warring with one another. The mightiest of all was the Hillands. The Hilland was ruled at the time by the cruel tyrant Edgar. Edgar Tabrizi eventually conquered the northern realm, and took the opposing king's daughter as a wife, Regina. Regina was already in love with the man who would become Bori, captain of her father's guard. Bori was a kind and just man who believed in freedom and good will towards all.

He publicly challenged Edgar to a fight, and being a prideful man, Edgar accepted. Being a deceitful man, he also cheated. As he began to lose the combat, Edgar had arranged for his servants to threaten the life of Regina to force Bori to throw the fight. Bori agreed to throw the fight, but Regina was killed anyway. witnessing the death of his beloved, Bori grew so powerful he became a God and slay Edgar where he stood. This is how the Wychwood came to be, as Bori's anger destroyed all life within the nearby woods.

Bori became the new ruler of the realm and in witnessing his goodness and power, became the primary god of the region.

Boriell Today

Boriell is ruled by a council of elders called "The Four" each chosen at birth by as a reincarnation of his spirit. When the old dies, a new one is born, and a search begins for the new member. Each of the four oversees an area directly, as indicated above, and this is where the new child is born.

  • The Hands: Silas
  • The Eyes: Ostego
  • The Heart: Hiltonia
  • The Mouth: Guin

The council convenes monthly to oversee and matters of the realm, including: major trials, new dangers, political treaties, as well as matters of budget. The last noteable trial the council oversaw was that of Hawa Parra, who attempted to overthrow the government & lost in a fight with Bori himself.
